Course Management

Courses form the basis of an online course registration system. Every course should have a course title, course text books, and reference books. Every course can have multiple sections offered in different semesters (fall, summer and spring) in any particular year. A course may belong to a single department or multiple departments. Only students who belong to those departments can register for a section of the course Management System.

Section Management

Every section belongs to a specific course in a specific semester of a specific year. Every section should have a maximum number of slots for the students to register. Any section can be taught by a single professor. Every section should have a class room and time span assigned for it. A section can be taught either on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays OR on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Student addmit those section as his wish...
